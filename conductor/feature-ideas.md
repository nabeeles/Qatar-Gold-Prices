# Future Development Ideas & Roadmap: Qatar Gold Prices

This document outlines researched feature enhancements to transform the application from a price tracker into a comprehensive financial tool tailored for the Qatari market.

## 1. Market-Leading Comparison & Arbitrage Tools
*   **"Best Buy Today" Badge:** 
    *   **Description:** Automatically identify and highlight the retailer (Al Fardan, Joyalukkas, Malabar, etc.) offering the lowest price for each karat.
    *   **Value:** Saves users time from manual comparison and provides immediate utility.
*   **Expats' Remittance vs. Gold Arbitrage:**
    *   **Description:** A calculator comparing the cost of buying gold in Qatar (QAR) versus remitting that cash home (e.g., to India, Pakistan, Philippines) and buying gold there.
    *   **Value:** Targets the 85% expat population in Qatar using real-time exchange rates and international gold rates.
*   **Jewelry Price Estimator (Making Charges):**
    *   **Description:** A slider allowing users to add "Making Charges" (typically 3%–20% in Qatar) to the base gold price.
    *   **Value:** Provides a realistic final price before the user visits a showroom.

## 2. Essential Financial & Religious Tools
*   **Zakat Calculator:**
    *   **Description:** Calculate the annual Zakat (2.5%) based on the user's total gold weight once it exceeds the *Nisab* threshold (approx. 85g of 24K gold).
    *   **Value:** Essential cultural and religious utility for the local and Muslim expat community.
*   **Gold Savings Plan Tracker:**
    *   **Description:** Features to track monthly payments and accumulated weight in popular jeweler savings schemes (e.g., Malabar Gold's Smart Buy).
    *   **Value:** Increases app retention as users log in monthly to update their progress.
*   **Avios Rewards Estimator:**
    *   **Description:** Integrate Al Fardan Exchange’s reward logic (e.g., 250 Avios per ounce of gold bullion).
    *   **Value:** Leverages the local synergy between Al Fardan and Qatar Airways.

## 3. Localization & Regional Specifics
*   **21K Gold Support:** 
    *   **Description:** Update database schema and UI to include 21K gold, which is highly popular in local Qatari and Arabic designs.
*   **Arabic Language Support:**
    *   **Description:** Full Right-to-Left (RTL) UI for the local Qatari and Arab expat community.
*   **VAT Preparedness Toggle:**
    *   **Description:** A "Future VAT" toggle to show estimated prices with a 5% VAT (expected in 2026).

## 4. Personalization & Retention
*   **Digital Vault (Portfolio):**
    *   **Description:** Allow users to record their physical gold purchases (Weight, Karat, Date Bought). Show "Unrealized Profit/Loss" based on today's rates.
*   **Interactive Event Calendar:**
    *   **Description:** Countdown and alerts for major gold-buying events like the **Doha Jewelry & Watches Exhibition (DJWE)**, Ramadan, and Eid.
*   **Glanceable Home Screen Widgets:**
    *   **Description:** iOS/Android widgets showing the latest 22K/24K rates without opening the app.
